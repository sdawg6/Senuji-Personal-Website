About Me

Welcome to my personal travel diary! This space is all about sharing the places that have captured my heart and those that are still on my bucket list (because why not dream big?). From the incredible cultures I’ve experienced, to the flavors I’ve savored, and the amazing people I’ve met along the way — each adventure holds a special memory. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to email me. Also, if you're planning your next trip, I highly recommend checking out Expedia.ca for great travel deals and recommendations. I'll be sharing pictures from each of my journeys so you can see the world through my eyes. Join me as I take you through my adventures, and invite you to dream alongside me about the adventures still to come.

These are my three must-have travel essentials, no matter where I’m traveling. My Kodak film camera is my window to the world, capturing every special moment you see in this diary with a nostalgic, timeless touch. I never leave home without my AirPods, as music is my travel companion, turning any journey into a soundtrack of memories. And I NEVER forget my Vaseline — it’s my secret weapon for everything from dry lips to random travel emergencies. These three essentials are all I need to stay ready for anything.
